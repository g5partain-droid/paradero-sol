The challenge — inherent to how hospitality systems are built
Guest history lives in Opera. Preferences live in WhatsApp. Spa bookings in Compaq. Every system is its own island.
The magic of a place like Paradero lives in the details — but today those details live in people's heads, not in a system that everyone can access.

Reviews, CRM signals, booking patterns — the intelligence is there. It just doesn't flow where it's needed.
